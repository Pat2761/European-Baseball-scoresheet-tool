Balk


A BALK is an illegal act by the pitcher with a runner or runners on base, entitling all runners to advance one base [OBR Definition of Terms].

Example 44: The abbreviation used for an advance on a balk is "BK" followed by the batting order number of the player at bat at the time of the balk. If there is more than one runner on base, "BK" (upper case) is used for the lead runner and "bk" (lower case) for the subsequent runners.
