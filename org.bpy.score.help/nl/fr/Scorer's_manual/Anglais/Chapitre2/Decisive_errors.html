Decisive errors

A decisive error is one that prolongs the life of an offensive player. In other words, a decisive error is charged only when we are certain that the defense would have scored a putout if it had not been committed.

This type of error is recorded on the score-sheet with an "E" followedby the symbol for the fielder who committed the error, and, if applicable, proceeded by the fielders who made assists.

There are different ways of recording a decisive error:

  • Error fielding a ground ball E.

  • Error fielding a thrown ball _E.

  • Throwing error E.T

  • Error on fly E.F

  • Error on line drive E.L

  • Error on pop fly E.P
