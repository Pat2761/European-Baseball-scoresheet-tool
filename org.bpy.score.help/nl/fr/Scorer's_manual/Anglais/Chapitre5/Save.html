This statistic is given according to the provisions of rule 9.19 of the OBR:

The official scorer shall credit a pitcher with a save when such pitcher meets all four of t he following conditions:

  • He is the finishing pitcher in a game won by his team;

  • He is not the winning pitcher;

  • He is credited with at least 1/3 of an inning pitched; and

  • He satisfies one of the following conditions:

    • He enters the game with a lead of no more t han three runs and pitches for at least one inning;

      • He enters the game, regardless of the c ount, with the potential tying run either on base, or at bat, or on deck (that is, the potential t ying run is either already on base or is one of the first two batters he faces); or

        • He pitches for at least three innings.
